Privacy Policy — Pawtfolio

Effective date: March 19, 2026

Introduction

Nextia ("we", "us", or "our") built Pawtfolio as a mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy — we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

Information the App Uses

Pawtfolio accesses certain device capabilities solely to provide its core features. All of your data remains on your device:

  • Notifications
    Used to send you reminders for vaccinations, vet appointments, and pet care events. Notification scheduling is handled entirely on your device and no data is sent to any server.
  • Purchase Information
    If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) and by our payment partner RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details. RevenueCat may process an anonymous app user ID to manage your subscription. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not send your pet data to any server
  • We do not upload photos or documents to any external service
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track you
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data

Data Storage & Deletion

All your pet profiles, health records, documents, and app settings are stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data:

  • Delete any pet profile, record, or document within the app at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data from your device
  • We do not maintain any cloud backup of your data

Third-Party Services

Pawtfolio uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscriptions and purchases. Processes anonymous transaction data. Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — handles app distribution and payment processing according to their own privacy policies

Children's Privacy

Pawtfolio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, there is no risk of children's data being compromised.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
