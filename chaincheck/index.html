ChainCheck

Smart bike maintenance & ride tracking

ChainCheck is your all-in-one cycling companion. Track your rides with GPS, monitor component wear, and keep your bike in top shape with smart maintenance reminders powered by real sensor data.

Features

  • GPS ride tracking with detailed route recording
  • Manage multiple bikes in your digital garage
  • Track component wear and replacement schedules
  • Ride analytics — distance, speed, elevation, and trends
  • Smart maintenance alerts using gyroscope and accelerometer data
  • Weather and road condition logging
