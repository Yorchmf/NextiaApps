Privacy Policy — ChainCheck

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Introduction

Nextia ("we", "us", or "our") built ChainCheck as a mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy — we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

Information the App Uses

ChainCheck accesses certain device data solely to provide its core features. All of this data remains on your device:

  • Location Data (GPS)
    Used to track your cycling rides and record GPS routes. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.
  • Gyroscope & Accelerometer
    Used to detect cycling patterns and determine when bike maintenance may be needed. This sensor data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.
  • Purchase Information
    If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play) and by our payment partner RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not send your location data to any server
  • We do not transmit sensor data (gyroscope, accelerometer) anywhere
  • We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs that track you
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data

Data Storage & Deletion

All your ride data, bike information, and app settings are stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data:

  • Delete any ride, bike, or component data within the app at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data from your device
  • We do not maintain any cloud backup of your data

Third-Party Services

ChainCheck uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscriptions and purchases. Processes anonymous transaction data. Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — handles app distribution and payment processing according to their own privacy policies

Children's Privacy

ChainCheck is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, there is no risk of children's data being compromised.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
